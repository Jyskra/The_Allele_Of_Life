# The Allele Of Life
### More like a simulation than an actual game. Definitely not a copycat of the Conway's Game of Life with slight tweaks. 
### Since the base Game of Life is too boring to watch, my version introduces actual, more real-world like behaviour of the cells. That means you can enable and see how different individual settings impact the evolution of those cells. If something seems too powerful or too soft, it can be softened or increased respectfully.
### We all know reality hits hard, thats why when the simulation feels a bit too realistic, you can turn all influence off and enjoy the nice and cozy base Game of Life
